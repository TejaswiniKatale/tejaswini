About

A Computer Science engineer connecting AI methods with real-world infrastructure systems.

Hi! I am Tejaswini, a Master’s student in Computer Science at the University of Houston and a Research Assistant with Prof. Lu Gao in Civil and Environmental Engineering.

My interest in socially meaningful research started at home. My mother, a science teacher in India, and my father, a PhD scholar in social sciences, always emphasized how technology and science should come together to create a positive impact.

I am interested in building AI systems that make information easier to understand, help people make better decisions, and support social and environmental good.
